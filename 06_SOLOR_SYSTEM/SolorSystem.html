Solar System

Welcome to our solar system webpage! Here's some basic information about the planets:

  • Sun: The center of our solar system.
  • Mercury: The closest planet to the Sun.
  • Venus: The second planet from the Sun.
  • Earth: Our home planet.
  • Mars: The fourth planet from the Sun.
  • Jupiter: The largest planet in our solar system.
  • Saturn: Known for its stunning rings.
  • Uranus: A gas giant with a unique rotation.
  • Neptune: The eighth and farthest known planet from the Sun.
